## Investigation

### Root cause

Steering duoc viet voi intent state ("IoT sim la internal service -> nen di `/api/internal/*`") thay vi reality ("IoT sim hit `/mobile/*` direct").

Co the 2 nguyen nhan:

1. **Steering duoc draft theo ADR-004 target state** (prefix `/api/v1/{domain}/*`) nhung dung `/api/internal/*` sai - ADR-004 noi chuan la `/api/v1/{domain}/*`, khong phai `/api/internal/*`.
2. **Steering confuse internal route vs internal caller** - HealthGuard BE co `/api/v1/internal/websocket/emit-*` (dung la internal path). IoT sim -> health_system BE thi khong phai internal path, chi la "internal caller" dung `X-Internal-Service` header.

Path 6 trong `topology_v2.md` da note dung: "Base URL uses `/mobile/admin` WITHOUT `/api/v1` prefix". Steering sync khong update theo.
